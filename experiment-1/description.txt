**STUDENT REGISTRATION FORM TEST CASES & VALIDATIONS**

1. **FIELD: Name**
   **Requirement:**

   * This field is mandatory (`required`) – form cannot be submitted if it's empty.
   * Only alphabetic characters and spaces are allowed (`pattern="[A-Za-z\s]+"`).
   * `+` ensures at least one valid character must be present.

   **VALID INPUTS:**

   * `"Ujjwal Sharma"`
   * `"John Doe"`

   **INVALID INPUTS:**

   * `"ujjwal123"` – Numbers are not allowed.
   * `"ujjwal_sharma"` – Underscore is invalid.
   * `"UJJ@Sharma"` – Special characters like `@` are not permitted.

2. **FIELD: Email**
   **Requirement:**

   * Required field.
   * Must follow the valid email pattern (`type="email"`), e.g., `name@domain.com`.

   **VALID INPUTS:**

   * `"ujjwal@example.com"`
   * `"student123@college.edu"`

   **INVALID INPUTS:**

   * `"ujjwal"` – Missing `@` and domain.
   * `"ujjwal@.com"` – Missing domain name.
   * `"ujjwal@domain,com"` – Comma used instead of a period.

3. **FIELD: Age**
   **Requirement:**

   * Required field.
   * Must be a number (`type="number"`).
   * Age must be within the range `min="5"` and `max="130"`.

   **VALID INPUTS:**

   * `5` – Minimum allowed.
   * `21`, `50` – Typical values.
   * `130` – Maximum allowed.

   **INVALID INPUTS:**

   * `""` (empty) – Triggers required field error.
   * `4` – Less than minimum.
   * `131` – Greater than maximum.
   * `"abc"` – Not a numeric value.

4. **FORM SUBMISSION:**

   * If **all fields are valid**, the form submits successfully.
   * If **any field is invalid**, submission is blocked and browser displays validation messages.

**CSS STYLING BREAKDOWN**

A summary of the styling properties applied via CSS for visual design and layout:

1. **ELEMENT: body (Page Layout)**

   * `background-color: #c8a2c8;` (light purple): Sets a calming background tone.
   * Centering achieved via flexbox or vertical margin like `margin-top: 30vh;`.

2. **ELEMENT: form (Form Box)**

   * `background-color: #fffdd0;` (cream background): Highlights form against page.
   * `padding`, `border-radius`, and `border: ridge 2px green;`: Makes the form clean and approachable.
   * `margin` and centering properties ensure it stays visually balanced on screen.

3. **ELEMENT: input fields**

   * Full width with padding: Enhances usability.
   * `font-size: 17px;`, `border: 1px solid #ddd;`, `border-radius: 5px;`: Provides consistent and user-friendly input fields.

4. **ELEMENT: Submit Button (`<button type="submit">`)**

   * `background-color: green;`, `color: white;`: High contrast for visibility.
   * `font-size: 20px;`, `padding: 12px;`: Easy to spot and click.
   * `margin-top: 10px;`: Adds spacing from input fields.
